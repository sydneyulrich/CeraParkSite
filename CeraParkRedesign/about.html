Come with us.


We offer a range of campsites, from primitive to electric. Our differing lot sizes allow for all camper sizes. Our showers and restrooms are located at the heart of the campground, for close proximity to every campsite.


Come out and swim in one of our lifeguard-monitored pools. We have a large pool, slides and a kiddie pool. Pool use is included with your stay!


Looking for something new to do while camping? Try out one of our many amenities: including 18 holes of mini-golf, a ball field and multiple playgrounds throughout Cera Park.


Try your hand at fishing on the Wisconsin River. Whether you're fishing from shore, off one of our nine docks, or bring your own boat, we have a prime location to reel in your next fish!
